    EQUIPMENTS

    SS Grade

    ZEUS WAND

    Rare : Hurls Zeus Wand at 1 empty tile to deal damage within 3*3 range and leaves behind 1 Wand Mirage. 1 Divine Bolt is fired at random monsters for each ball that passes through Wand Mirange.

    Epic : Attack +20%

    Legendary : Additional chance to hurl 1 Wand

    Honor : Attack +30%

    Eternal : When balls pass through Wand Mirage, mirage fires 2 Divine Bolts

    ZEUS CROWN

    Rare : When monsters die, release a circular Divine Arcbolt 3*3 that deals damage to monsters hit.

    Epic : HP +20%

    Legendary : Devine Arcbolt instantly kills monsters below 10% HP when dealing damage

    Honor : HP +30%

    Eternal : Divine Arcbolt has a chance to inflict Vulnerability on monsters for 1 round when dealing damage

    S Grade

    POSEIDON WAND

    Rare : Unleashes a wave at 3 enemy columnsfor slight damage. If character's HP is less than 30%, enemies are knocked back.

    Epic : Attack +15%

    Legendary : The wave can freeze monsters.

    Honor : Attack +20%

    Eternal : Wave has 50% chance to become a tsunami and deal damage to all monsters.

    ATHENA ROB

    Rare : Get a shield with(Max HP * 5%) HP each turn. The shield lasts one round.

    Epic : HP +15%

    Legendary : All healing effects +20%.

    Honor : HP +20%

    Eternal : If Athena's shield is not broken this round, gain 20% attack next round.

    DEMETER RING

    Rare : 10% chance to add 1 Normal ball when a monster dies (Max10).

    Epic : Attack +15%

    Legendary : Slight increase in item drop rate when monster dies.

    Honor : Attack +20%

    Eternal : 1% chance to add 1 Special ball(Max 5) when a monster dies.

    HADES BRACERS

    Rare : Kills deal damage to all monsters. Damage is doubled with more than 5 kills.

    Epic : Attack 15%

    Legendary : When monsters die, restores 1% of max HP.

    Honor : Attack 20%.

    Eternal : Hades' Spirit Charge instantly kills monsters with less than 10% HP when it deals damage.

    ARES CROWN

    Rare : Kill 5+ monsters in 1 turn to gain 5% Max HP(MAX 50%)

    Epic : HP +15%.

    Legendary : When HP reaches cap, gain 15% attack.

    Honor : HP +20%.

    Eternal : Kill 3+ enemies in 1 turn to gain 2% attack(Max 20%)

    HERMES BOOTS

    Rare : Dodge +10%.

    Epic : HP +15%

    Legendary : Restores 5% of Max HP when dodging.

    Honor : HP +20%

    Eternal : Attack increased by 2% on dodge (max 20%)
