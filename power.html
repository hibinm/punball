    SKILL TIER LIST

    S Tier


    The S Tier are the best in class skills in almost all circumstances

    REVIVE : The best skill, it will revive you with full health and also wipe out enemies in the first 3 rows in front of you.

    SHADOW CLONE : Every 30 combo it will create a strong combos with your current selection of special balls.

    INVINCIBLE COVER : It will completely block any damage during the turn this skill is active.( If you are using tutle and this skill is activated along with the octopus dodge skill, you can force close the app, and the turn will move down by one turn, meaning your invinvible cover will be activated a turn before the octopus dodge skill)

    A Tier


    Good option skills, and can select as per the situation, like the enemy you are facing

    MISSILE BALL : Deals 3X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : It also fires an extra missile to the weakest enemy on each contact with same 3X damage.

    SPLIT BALL : Split ball deals 3X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : When it hits an enemy it will split into 4 little balls each with 1X damage of your normal ball.

    FROZEN BALL : Deals 1X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Have a chance to freeze an enemy.

    UPGRADE ICE BALL : 2 normal balls are upgraded to frozen balls.
    Effect : Have a chance to freeze an enemy.

    UPGRADE MISSILE : 2 normal balls are upgraded to missile balls with 3X your damage.
    Effect : It also fires an extra missile to the weakest enemy on each contact with same 3X damage.

    MISSILE MASTER : Increase missile damage by 40%.
    Effect : 2X Missiles are created at contact to enemy instead of one.

    UPGRADE LIGHTNING BALL : 2 normal balls are upgraded to lightning balls with 2X your normal damage.
    Effect : On hit to an enemy, it will also do lightning damage to 2 random adjacent enemies with 2X your normal damage.

    UPGRADE LASER : 2 normal balls are upgraded to laser balls whih deals 2X damage of your normal balls.
    Effect : On contact, hits all the enemies in the same row or column with 2X of your normal ball damage.

    B Tier


    B Tier skills are decent skills but not top of the line. Mostly are circumstancial to your previous skills or gear.

    SPECIAL BALL UP : Increases speacial ball damage by 20%.

    H-LASER BALL : Deals 2X damage of your normal balls.
    Effect : On contact, hits all the enemies in the same row with 2X of your normal ball damage.

    LIGHTNING BALL : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : On hit to an enemy, it will also do lightning damage to 2 random adjacent enemies with 2X your normal damage.

    V-LASER BALL : Deals 2X damage of your normal balls.
    Effect : On contact, hits all the enemies in the same column with 2X of your normal ball damage.

    SUPER ENTRY : Deals 3X damage of your normal or special ball which is fired first.

    DESTRUCTION BALL : Deals 20X damage of your normal ball.

    UPGRADE FIREBALL : 2 normal balls are upgraded to fire balls with 2X your normal damage.
    Effect : Enemies get burn effect for 2 turns, which gets 2X damage each turn.

    HEADSHOT BALL : Deals 1X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Chance to instantly kill an enemy of contact. First 5 rooms have 100% kill chance.

    ICE FROST : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Drop ice cone every 40 combo, also has chance to freeze enemies.

    DEATH MISSILE : Deals 3X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Deals multiple missile on death of enemy.

    MISSILE CHASER : Deals 8X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Every 50 hit fires a missile to the weakest enemy.

    BLACK HOLE BALL : Deals 20X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Always strikes first

    C Tier


    C Tier skills are lesser preffered than B Tier, still have good use.

    ICE MASTER : Deals 100% more ICE damage.
    Effect : Inccreased freezing chance.

    ATTACK UP : Normal and Special ball damage increased by 10%.

    LASER CUT COMBO : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Every 50 hit, fires a laser which deals 2X damage to all the enemies in a column.

    LASER SWEEP COMBO : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Every 50 hit, fires a laser which deals 2X damage to all the enemies in a row.

    FIRE BALL : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Enemies get burn effect for 2 turns, which gets 2X damage each turn.

    THUNDER BOLD : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Hits a random target and 2 nearby enemies every 40 hits with 2X damage.

    NORMAL BALL UP : Increases normal ball damage by 20%.

    BACKSTAB BALL : Deals 15X damage of your normal ball when hitting from back of the enemy, 1X from front and side.

    COMBO BOMB : Deals 5X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Every 100 combo drops a bomb, target and nearby enemies get the same damage.

    RECOVERY UP : Healing by the healing orb increased by 50%.

    DOUBLE EFFECT : Combo hits 2 times instead of 1 for Special balls. Cloned ball counts as 1 combo only.

    D Tier


    D Tier skills are not so powerful, to choose if no other good selection.

    DEATH LIGHTNING : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : On death deals damage to 4 random enemies.

    DEATH LASER : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : When enemy dies, it fires a laser and deals 2X damage on the same row.

    COMBO FIREBALL : Deals 2X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Drops a fireball every 40 hit. Enemy is burnt for 2X damage for 2 turns.

    BOMB BALL : Deals 6X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Explosion damages to surrounding enemies in 3X3 area. Always goes first or after Blackhole ball.

    SNIPER BALL : Deals 7X damage to 7th tile monsters, 6X to 6th tile and so on...
    Effect : Higher damage to further away enemies.

    COMBAT BALL : Deals 7X damage to first tile, 6X to second and so on.
    Effect : Higher damage to closer enemies.

    SPECIAL COMBO X 5 : Combo hits 4 times instead of 1 for Special balls. Cloned ball counts as 1 combo only.
    Effect :

    STRENGTH : Max HP + 5% every 5 rounds.

    F Tier


    F Tier skills are , well just meh...

    DRILL BALL : Deals 5X damage of your normal ball.
    Effect : Penetrates all enemies, bounce of walls. Always fired last.

    FINAL CHARGE : Deals 3X damage of your normal or special ball which is fired last.

    HP UP : Increases Max HP by 20%.

    LESS IS MORE : Combo requirement decreased by 10%.
